Vegetable Soup

Ingredient Amount
Spinach 2kg
Lamb's Lettuce 250g
Assorted Meat 2kg
Stock fish 1kg
Crayfish 4 tbls
Knorr Cubes 6 pcs
Sweet pepper for color
Habanero pepper to taste
Onion bulbs 5 pcs
Palm oil 11/2 cups
Salt to taste

Details: A delicious pot of vegetable soup for dinner with family.

  1. Cook the meats and stock fish with the seasoning cubes and half of the chopped onions.
  2. When done, remove all the meat and fish and set aside.
  3. De-bone and separate the stockfish into small pieces.
  4. Make sure the stock is as little as possible, if not, boil it down first before continuing.
  5. Set the stock on the stove, add palm oil, crayfish, the remaining chopped onions. Cover and cok for about 7 minutes to allow the palm oil integrate into the soup.
  6. Add the peppers, the lambs lettuce, the meats and fish.
  7. Cover and once it boils again, add the spinach.
  8. Cover and once it boils again, stir very well and add salt if necessary.
  9. Transfer the soup to cool containers immediately so the vegetables do not change colour.

Porridge Yam with vegetables

Ingredient Amount
Yam 1 tuber
Palm oil 11/2 cups
Smoked fish 1
Grounded Crayfish 1 tbls
Onion 1 medium
Green vegetable 2kg
Habanero pepper To taste
Salt To taste
Knorr Cubes 5pcs

Details: Mouth-watering Homemade Yam Porridge for lunch

  1. Peel & cut the yam tuber into medium sizes. Wash the yam cubes and place in a sizeable pot.
  2. Wash & cut the onions into tiny pieces. Grind / Blend the chilli pepper. Set these aside. If using dry fish, soak and pick the bones at this time.
  3. Pour enough water to cover the yam cubes and start cooking at medium to high heat. If you are using dry fish, add it now.
  4. When the yams have got a good boil, add the onions, ground crayfish, pepper, Knorr cubes, palm oil & the smoked fish.
  5. Cover the pot and continue cooking till the yam is done. Add salt to taste and stir very well. Cook at high heat for about 5 minutes.
  6. Add the green vegetable, stir and leave to stand for about 3 minutes then serve!

Recipe source: Example Poridge Yam Recipe

Okro Soup

Ingredient Amount
Fresh okro 1 big bowl
Stockfish head 200g
Beef 1kg
Frozen Mackerel 400g
Seasoning cubes 3
Scotch Bonnet Peppers 3
Palm Oil 200ml
Ground Crayfish 1/2 cup

Details: A delicious Pot of Okro Soup to be ate with a well made prepared Swallow.

  1. Season the beef and mackerel and cook together, remove the mackerel and continue to cook the meat till it becomes soft.
  2. Add the washed stockfish and add to the cooking meat, allow to cook for another 10 minutes.
  3. Add ground crayfish, palm oil, 1 seasoning cube, pepper and salt to taste.
  4. The sliced okra should be added at this point, stir. drop the cooked mackerel fish on top.
  5. Allow to simmer for 3-4 minutes and you are done with this delicious soup.
  6. Okra soup can be paired with fufu or eba.

Recipe source: Example Okro Soup Recipe
